Our Story

In Manila City ChicClick! Nail Salon first opened its doors to the public, establishing itself as a premier destination for exquisite nail care and artistry. Founded by Jariah Camille Rivera, Zerylyn Siena, Melody Tapay, and Joanna Micah Villegas, ChicClick! offers exceptional nail services and customer-centric approach.

The founders and employeers, with extensive training and expertise in the field of nail care and beauty, brought together their passion for nail artistry and a commitment to customer satisfaction. Their dedication to excellence has set ChicClick! to consistently deliver high quality services that exceed both business and customer standards.

At ChicClick!, we take pride in our employees' exemplary skills and the wide range of services we offer. From classic manicures and pedicures to luxurious nail enhancements and stunning nail art, our skilled technicians are dedicated to helping you achieve the perfect look that reflects your unique style and personality.
